5 Pillars of Islam

  • Shahada (Faith)

    The declaration of faith in the oneness of Allah and the prophethood of Muhammad.

  • Salat (Prayer)

    The performance of ritual prayers facing the Kaaba in Mecca, five times a day.

  • Zakat (Charity)

    The giving of alms to those in need, typically calculated as a percentage of one's wealth.

  • Sawm (Fasting)

    The observance of fasting during the holy month of Ramadan as a means of self-purification.

  • Hajj (Pilgrimage)

    The pilgrimage to the holy city of Mecca, which is obligatory for every Muslim who is able to undertake it.
